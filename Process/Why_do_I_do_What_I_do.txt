==========
Investing:
==========
1) Why do I Invest ?
	
	* To stop working for money and start working on this that I want to and need to.
	* To inspire others with what I have learned and ask them to join me.
	* To unlock the financial genius inside me and others.
	* For the joy of seeing my knowledge and wealth grow. 

2) How do I Invest ?
	* I intend to compuond my wealth at aleast CAGR 25 % for next 20 years.
	* I invest directly in equities for capital gains and not for dividends.
	* I don't diversify rather I pick one company and hold on to it till it keeps growing.
	* If my pick does not perform as expected within the time frame that I defined, then I 
	  sell my position in that stock, minimizing my losses and move on to the next stock.
	* I pick a stock based on following fundamentals,
		1) Potential for Growth
		2) Quality of Management
		3) Storng Balancesheet
		4) Availability of credit on both production and consumption sides
		5) Customer sentiment for products that company sells
	* I use technical analysis to calibrate entry and exits of my positions.
	* I monitor Quaterly results, read Annual Reports, read Earning conference call updates
	  and generally monitor most of the corporate actions taken by the company and follow
	  all the news that is related to the company periodically and take my buy and sell 
	  decisions based on above actions.
